## Guidelines
- Each tweet MUST be 280 characters or fewer. Count carefully.
- The thread should contain no more than 15 tweets.
- First tweet is the hook — it must stand alone and grab attention.
- Number each tweet (1/, 2/, ...) at the start for thread continuity.
- Use the last tweet for a summary, call-to-action, or link placeholder.
- Avoid mid-word line breaks; keep sentences complete within a single tweet.

## Tone
- Concise and punchy — match the style profile where possible.
- Use plain language; avoid dense academic phrasing.
- Emojis are acceptable sparingly if they fit the author's style.
