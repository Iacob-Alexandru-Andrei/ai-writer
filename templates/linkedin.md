## Guidelines
- Start with a strong hook in the first line (max 210 characters) to capture
  attention before the "see more" fold.
- Keep the total post under 3000 characters.
- Use short paragraphs (1-3 sentences) separated by blank lines.
- Incorporate line breaks for readability on mobile.
- End with a question or call-to-action to encourage engagement.
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags at the end.

## Tone
- Professional yet personable — match the style profile above.
- Avoid jargon unless the audience expects it.
- Write in first person where appropriate.
